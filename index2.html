About Me

Summary

I am an enthusiastic problem-solver with a strong grasp of the entire software development life cycle, having worked as a project manager, lead developer, and assistant developer across both professional and personal projects. Since beginning my software development journey in 2015 and gaining professional experience since 2019, I have completed over 1,000 tickets completed for organizations such as mThree, JPMChase, and MaxProjections. I have over 10 years of development experience. I am well-versed in enterprise development, specializing in the .NET ecosystem, cloud architecture, and AI-powered workflows. I regularly challenge myself to learn new skills and adapt quickly to emerging paradigms, and I am eager to leverage my expertise to contribute to a dynamic team.

Leadership Approach

As a team leader, I adopt a hands-off approach focused on empowering my team to grow and succeed. My leadership style involves breaking down overall projects into manageable subtasks and delegating them based on priority. For high-priority tasks, I assign team members according to their specific skills to ensure efficiency and speed up development. In contrast, for low-priority tasks, I delegate responsibilities that challenge teammates by placing them slightly outside their current skill set, fostering growth and skill enhancement. I remain accessible to provide guidance and support when needed, promoting a learning environment while reinforcing team cohesion. This structured delegation model allows me to prioritize both task completion and the professional development of each team member, ultimately creating a strong and versatile team ready to tackle any challenge.
